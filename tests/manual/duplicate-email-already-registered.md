# Duplicate email (already registered)

## Preconditions
An account already exists with the email "existing@example.com".

## Steps
1. Fill in a valid username and password.
2. Enter "existing@example.com" as the email.
3. Submit.

## Expected Result
The form is rejected with an "email already in use" error. No duplicate account is created.

## Severity
Major
